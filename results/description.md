This directory contains the three visualizations which were required for analysis. Their descriptions are below:  

1. `Number of Fires vs. Distance from Salina`: Produce a histogram showing the number of fires occurring every 50 mile distance from Salina up to the max specified distance.  
2. `Total Acres Burned`: Produce a time series graph of total acres burned per year for the fires occurring in the specified distance from Salina.  
3. `Fire Smoke Estimate and AQI Estimate for Salina`: Produce a time series graph containing fire smoke estimate and the AQI estimate for Salina.
